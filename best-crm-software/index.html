Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best CRM for small businesses? +

HubSpot CRM is our top recommendation for small businesses due to its completely free tier with unlimited contacts, intuitive interface, and excellent customer support. Freshsales is another great option if you want built-in calling and email features. Both offer affordable pricing as your team grows.

Can I use a CRM for free? +

Yes! HubSpot CRM offers a completely free plan with unlimited contacts and core CRM features. Zoho CRM also offers a free tier with limited features. However, free plans typically lack advanced automation, custom reports, and integrations that require paid tiers.

How much does a CRM typically cost? +

CRM pricing varies widely based on features and scale. Budget CRMs like Monday Sales start at $9/user/month. Mid-market options like Pipedrive and Freshsales range from $15-40/user/month. Enterprise solutions like Salesforce start at $165/user/month. Most offer discounts for annual billing and additional users.

Which CRM is easiest to use? +

HubSpot CRM receives our highest marks for ease of use with its clean, intuitive interface and excellent onboarding process. Pipedrive is also highly user-friendly with its visual pipeline management. Monday Sales CRM offers excellent customization and visual appeal. The "easiest" choice depends on your specific needs and team background.

Can I switch CRM systems once I start using one? +

Yes, switching CRM systems is possible but requires planning. Most CRMs offer data export capabilities and migration tools. Consider timing your migration carefully to minimize disruption. Many CRM vendors offer migration assistance and can import data from competing platforms. Start with a pilot group before full migration.
