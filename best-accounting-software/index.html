Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best accounting software for small businesses? +

QuickBooks Online and Xero are our top recommendations for small businesses. QuickBooks Online offers the most comprehensive features and industry support, while Xero excels with an intuitive interface and growing business flexibility. FreshBooks is perfect if you're a freelancer or service provider. For budget-conscious startups, Wave offers completely free accounting functionality that covers essential needs.

Is there free accounting software? +

Yes! Wave offers completely free accounting software with invoicing, expense tracking, financial reporting, and receipt scanning. FreshBooks provides a free core plan with basic functionality. Zoho Books offers a limited free tier. However, free accounting software typically lacks advanced features like payroll, advanced reporting, or premium integrations that come with paid plans.

What features are most important in accounting software? +

The most important features depend on your business, but generally include: invoicing and bill management, bank reconciliation, expense tracking, financial reporting, tax compliance support, mobile access, and integration with your other business tools. For service businesses, time tracking matters. For e-commerce, inventory management is critical. For enterprises, multi-subsidiary support and advanced consolidation become essential.

Can accounting software help with tax preparation? +

Yes, accounting software significantly streamlines tax preparation. QuickBooks Online and Xero track expenses by tax category, calculate estimated taxes, and facilitate integration with tax software. They maintain audit trails and generate tax reports that simplify year-end filing. While accounting software handles organization and calculation, professional tax advice from a CPA is still recommended for complex situations.

How much does accounting software typically cost? +

Accounting software pricing ranges widely. Free options like Wave cost nothing. Budget options like Zoho Books start at $9/month. Mid-range solutions like Xero start at $13/month and QuickBooks Online at $30/month. Premium tiers and additional users increase costs. Enterprise solutions like NetSuite require custom pricing. Most platforms charge more for advanced features, additional users, or payroll processing.

What's the difference between QuickBooks and Xero? +

QuickBooks Online offers more comprehensive features and stronger U.S. tax integration, making it better for complex accounting needs. Xero features a more intuitive interface, better real-time collaboration, and stronger international support. QuickBooks typically costs more but provides more features. Xero is often better for growing businesses prioritizing ease of use and collaboration. Both are excellent choices; the best depends on your specific needs and priorities.

Can I switch accounting software once I start using one? +

Yes, switching is possible but requires planning. Most accounting software allows data export and many platforms offer migration assistance or use standardized formats like CSV for data transfer. Plan the switch during a natural accounting period, like at year-end. Consider having a brief overlap period with both systems. Many accountants can help manage the transition. The key is proper planning to maintain financial data integrity throughout the migration.
