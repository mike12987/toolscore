# ToolScore.io

> The Wirecutter of business software. Honest, curated SaaS reviews and comparisons.

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://toolscore-one.vercel.app)

## What is ToolScore?

ToolScore.io is an independent software comparison platform that helps businesses find the right tools. We provide transparent, data-driven reviews across 6 categories with detailed scoring on Features, Ease of Use, Pricing, Integrations, Support, and Mobile.

## Categories

- **CRM Software** - Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Monday, Freshsales, Close
- **Project Management** - Compare top PM tools for teams of all sizes
- **Email Marketing** - Find the right email platform for your business
- **Accounting Software** - Best accounting tools for small businesses
- **HR Software** - HRIS and HR management solutions
- **Marketing Automation** - Automate your marketing workflows

## Head-to-Head Comparisons

Direct comparison pages for popular matchups:
- HubSpot vs Salesforce
- Monday vs Asana
- Pipedrive vs HubSpot
- And 8+ more

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Static HTML, vanilla JavaScript
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **SEO:** Schema.org structured data, OpenGraph, sitemap.xml
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4
