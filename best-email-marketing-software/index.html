Email Marketing Software Buyer's Guide

What to Look for in Email Marketing Software

Choosing the right email marketing platform depends on your specific business needs, team size, and growth trajectory. Here are the key factors to consider when evaluating options:

1. Free Plan and Pricing Model

Consider whether you need to start with a free plan. Mailchimp, HubSpot, Brevo, and ConvertKit offer generous free plans with no credit card required. Understand the pricing model: some charge per contact (expensive at scale), while others charge monthly regardless of subscriber count. For growing businesses, usage-based pricing (like Klaviyo) may provide better value initially but can escalate quickly.

2. Automation Capabilities

Email automation is crucial for scaling your marketing efforts. ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo excel at sophisticated automation with conditional logic. Mailchimp offers excellent visual automation builders. Ensure the platform supports the workflows you need: welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, re-engagement campaigns, and lifecycle marketing.

3. Integration Ecosystem

Check how well the platform integrates with your existing tools. E-commerce users need Shopify/WooCommerce integrations. B2B teams need CRM connections. Content creators need WordPress integration. Mailchimp, HubSpot, and Brevo offer the broadest integration support with 1,000+ connected apps.

4. Segmentation and Personalization

Advanced segmentation enables sending relevant messages to the right audience. Look for platforms supporting behavioral segmentation, dynamic content, and AI-powered personalization. Klaviyo specializes in behavioral segmentation for e-commerce. HubSpot integrates CRM data for B2B personalization.

5. Template and Content Tools

Evaluate the quality of email templates, whether drag-and-drop editors are intuitive, and if the platform includes built-in landing pages or forms. Constant Contact offers industry-specific templates. ConvertKit provides creator-focused content tools. All major platforms offer responsive, mobile-friendly templates.

6. Analytics and Reporting

Understand what metrics matter for your business. All platforms track opens, clicks, and unsubscribes. E-commerce platforms like Klaviyo show revenue attribution. Advanced B2B platforms like ActiveCampaign provide pipeline influence tracking. Real-time reporting helps you optimize campaigns quickly.

7. Customer Support and Learning Resources

Constant Contact offers live phone support and local representatives. HubSpot provides extensive free courses and certification. Mailchimp and AWeber have robust knowledge bases. Consider whether you need phone support or if email/chat support is sufficient.

8. Compliance and Deliverability

Email reputation and deliverability matter more than features. All major platforms maintain good sender reputation, but some excel at this. ConvertKit is known for exceptional deliverability. Ensure the platform provides compliance tools for GDPR, CCPA, and CAN-SPAM requirements.

Getting Started: Step-by-Step

Frequently Asked Questions

Which email marketing platform is truly free forever? +

Mailchimp, HubSpot, Brevo, ConvertKit, and AWeber all offer free plans with no expiration date. Mailchimp and Brevo allow unlimited emails to up to 500/unlimited contacts. HubSpot and ConvertKit offer unlimited contacts on their free plans. AWeber limits free plans to 500 subscribers but allows unlimited automation. No credit card is required to start or stay on these free plans indefinitely.

What's the difference between email marketing platforms and email service providers? +

Email marketing platforms focus on campaigns, automation, and marketing workflows. Email service providers (ESP) handle the technical delivery of emails. Today, the terms are largely interchangeable. All platforms covered in this guide are both marketing platforms and ESPs, handling campaign creation and email delivery.

Can I switch email marketing platforms without losing my contacts? +

Yes, all major platforms support exporting your contact lists as CSV files. Most platforms also offer direct migration from competitors. Plan your switch carefully to avoid sending duplicate emails during transition. Consider running both systems in parallel for a few weeks. Mailchimp, HubSpot, and Brevo provide migration guides and support.

What email marketing platform is best for e-commerce? +

Klaviyo is the top choice for e-commerce with native Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, behavioral tracking, and revenue attribution. Mailchimp is also excellent for e-commerce and offers better free plan options. Constant Contact and Brevo provide good value for smaller online stores. The best choice depends on your store platform, team size, and budget.

How important is automation in email marketing? +

Automation is critical for scaling email marketing without overwhelming your team. Automated workflows like welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, and post-purchase follow-ups drive significant revenue with minimal manual work. All platforms reviewed here offer automation capabilities, but sophistication varies. ActiveCampaign and Klaviyo excel at complex automation for advanced users.

Do I need CRM integration with my email marketing platform? +

CRM integration enables better personalization by giving your email platform access to customer data. HubSpot's built-in CRM is excellent for aligned teams. Mailchimp and Brevo integrate with most external CRMs via API or integrations. For B2B companies, deeper CRM integration (HubSpot or ActiveCampaign) provides more value. For e-commerce, Klaviyo's customer data layer is purpose-built.

What's the difference between Mailchimp and HubSpot email marketing? +

Mailchimp is a focused email marketing platform with excellent automation at all price levels. HubSpot is an all-in-one platform with email, CRM, landing pages, and more integrated. Both offer free plans forever. Mailchimp is better if you only need email. HubSpot is better if you want CRM and marketing tools unified. HubSpot's free tier lacks automation; Mailchimp's includes it.

How do I improve email deliverability? +

Deliverability depends on sender reputation, list quality, and content. All platforms help by providing DKIM/SPF setup and bounce management. Best practices: maintain clean lists by removing inactive subscribers, avoid spam-triggering words, provide easy unsubscribes, and authenticate your domain. ConvertKit is known for exceptional deliverability. Platforms with dedicated deliverability support include Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign.
